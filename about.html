About Invenio Technologies

We build industrial technology platforms that give capital project teams real-time visibility across engineering, procurement, logistics, and field execution.

Our Mission

Capital projects lose millions to scattered data, manual processes, and blind spots across the supply chain. Invenio Technologies was founded to fix that. We combine deep construction and engineering expertise with AI-powered software to deliver a single source of truth — from purchase order to installation — so projects move faster, safer, and on budget.

We provide turnkey and custom solutions across Civil, Structural, Mechanical, Instrumentation, and Electrical disciplines, adapting our platforms to each client's specific needs while maintaining strict budget and deadline compliance.

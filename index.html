    What does Invenio Technologies offer?

    Invenio Technologies provides a suite of industrial software platforms: the Kindred AI Engineering Platform for automated design and compliance, the Material Status Report (MSR) dashboard for procurement and shipment tracking, and mobile asset management tools for field operations and laydown yard management.

    How does the Kindred AI platform work?

    The Kindred platform uses AI to analyze engineering documents, check compliance against codes and standards, identify design gaps, and generate optimized drawings. It manages work packages across eight engineering disciplines with real-time progress tracking and automated approval workflows.

    Can distributed teams use Invenio platforms?

    Yes. All Invenio platforms are cloud-based and accessible from anywhere. Field workers use our mobile apps for on-site scanning and inspections, while office staff and engineers access dashboards and AI tools from any browser. Real-time sync keeps everyone aligned regardless of location.

    What makes Invenio different from other project tools?

    Unlike generic project management tools, Invenio platforms are purpose-built for industrial capital projects. Our AI engine understands engineering standards, our tracking covers the full material lifecycle from PO to installation, and our dashboards give stakeholders real-time visibility without manual reporting.

    Does Invenio integrate with existing tools?

    Yes. Kindred integrates with AutoCAD Plant 3D and other CAD tools for real-time file synchronization. Our asset tracking platform connects with Samsara and other IoT providers. All platforms feature API access for integration with your existing ERP and project management systems.

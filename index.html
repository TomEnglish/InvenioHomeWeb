FAQ

Everything you need to know

What does Invenio Technologies offer?

An integrated platform for tracking materials, equipment, and project execution across energy and industrial builds — from design through commissioning. Our products cover asset tracking, shipment visibility, multi-discipline work packaging, and real-time dashboards.

How does the AI engineering platform work?

We combine structured project data (materials, drawings, schedules, test packages) with AI-assisted workflows that draft site plans, detect gaps, flag schedule risk, and automate compliance documentation — keeping the human engineer in the loop at every step.

Does Invenio integrate with existing tools?

Yes. We integrate with the ERPs, scheduling, document-control, and telematics systems our customers already run, and expose our own APIs and shareable links for downstream stakeholders.

Can distributed teams use Invenio platforms?

Yes. Our web and mobile surfaces are designed for field, office, and client use across sites and time zones, with offline-capable field workflows and role-scoped sharing.

What makes Invenio different from other project tools?

We treat material intelligence as the backbone of project visibility — not as an afterthought bolted onto a generic project-management tool. The result is one source of truth that procurement, engineering, and field crews all trust.
